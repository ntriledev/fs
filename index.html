General Questions

What is FlirtSpot?
FlirtSpot is a privacy-first, ephemeral proximity-matching app designed for real-world interactions. It allows users to discover people nearby and share a "spark" without leaving a digital footprint.
How do I create an account?
You don't. FlirtSpot is entirely anonymous. There are no phone numbers, emails, or social logins required. Your identity is a temporary ID generated locally on your device.
Why can't I change my gender after onboarding?
To ensure the integrity and safety of the ecosystem, gender selection is permanent. This selection determines your user mode (Man Mode or Woman Mode) and cannot be modified once saved.
What is the difference between Man Mode and Woman Mode?
Man Mode users (Discoverers) scan for nearby signals and initiate "Flirts." Woman Mode users (Publishers) broadcast a unique "Connection Tag" and decide which incoming Flirts to accept.
Is FlirtSpot only for people 18 and older?
Yes. FlirtSpot is strictly for adults (16+). We verify age via year of birth during onboarding to maintain a safe and mature environment.
🛡️

Privacy & Security

Is my data stored on a server?
No. We use a "Zero-Persistence" architecture. Our relay servers operate using volatile RAM only. Once you or your peer disconnects, all session data is instantly purged.
Can I take a screenshot of a received photo?
No. We use Android's FLAG_SECURE technology to block screenshots and screen recordings. If you try to capture an ephemeral photo, the resulting image will be solid black.
Are my messages encrypted?
Yes. Every interaction is End-to-End Encrypted (E2EE). Your phone encrypts the data using the recipient's public key, and only their device can decrypt it.
Does the app track my GPS location?
FlirtSpot uses proximity technology to find people nearby, but we do not track or log your absolute GPS coordinates on our servers.
What happens if I delete the app?
Because there are no central accounts, deleting the app wipes all local data (interaction logs, profile settings, and keys). You will effectively vanish from the FlirtSpot universe.
📡

Connectivity & Modes

What is P2P Mode?
P2P (Peer-to-Peer) Mode allows your phone to communicate directly with other devices using local hardware links. This bypasses the internet entirely.
Does FlirtSpot work without internet?
Yes! In P2P Mode, you can discover and interact with others in areas with zero cellular service, such as subways, festivals, or remote locations.
What is Relay Mode?
Relay Mode uses our secure, RAM-only server to help bridge connections when devices are not in direct hardware range but are on the same network or internet.
How do I switch between Relay and P2P modes?
You can switch your preferred communication mode at any time within the app settings. The app handles the transition seamlessly.
✨

Interactions & Usage

What is a "Connection Tag"?
In Woman Mode, you generate a Connection Tag — a unique, temporary alphanumeric string. Nearby users in Man Mode use this tag to find your "Spot" and send a Flirt.
What is a "Flirt"?
A Flirt is a spontaneous, ephemeral selfie sent to initiate a connection. It represents the "spark" of the moment.
Why do received photos disappear?
Every photo received has a 60-second timer. This encourages you to live in the moment and ensures your image doesn't linger on someone else's device.
What does it mean to "Twin"?
When a Flirt is accepted, your screens "Twin." This opens a temporary, encrypted chat where you can coordinate meeting in person.
How do I block or report someone?
You can block any user directly from the interaction screen. Because we don't store PII, blocking is handled locally on your device to prevent future signals from that Peer ID.
Is it safe to meet someone I find on the app?
FlirtSpot facilitates the initial connection, but you should always prioritize your safety. Meet in public, well-lit spaces and trust your instincts. The app's ephemeral nature is designed to lead to real-world conversation quickly.
