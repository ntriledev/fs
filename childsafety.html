🛡️ Child Safety Policy

A Line
We Never Cross.

FlirtSpot is built exclusively for adults aged 18 and over. There is no place for minors on this platform — and zero tolerance for anyone who would harm them. This page explains exactly how we protect children and how you can help.

Our Zero-Tolerance Stance

flirt.spot has a zero-tolerance policy towards Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation. Any such content or behavior is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate and permanent removal from the platform. This applies to every message, image, and piece of content on our service — including our ephemeral, anonymous features, and anonymity is never a shield from this rule.

Four Layers of Protection

How we keep minors off the app and abuse off the network.

Strictly 18+ Gate

Every account must certify it is operated by an adult by providing a year of birth that qualifies as 18 or older before the app can be used. FlirtSpot is not directed to, and may not be used by, anyone under 18.

age-gate · 18+ · on-device

Zero Tolerance for CSAM

Any content that sexualizes or exploits a minor results in immediate removal, permanent ban, and a report to the NCMEC CyberTipline and relevant law-enforcement authorities.

Reported to NCMEC

In-App Reporting

You can report any user directly from their profile. Our system monitors reports to identify and remove malicious actors — while maintaining your anonymity. Reports flagging minors or child exploitation are escalated to our highest priority queue and triaged immediately.

Anonymous · From Profile

Decisive, Documented Action

When we confirm a violation, we remove the content, terminate the account, and preserve evidence for authorities. We do not warn repeat offenders — they are gone.

remove · ban · preserve

Keeping Minors Out

Prevention is the first line of defense.

🔞 Age Gate at the Door

Onboarding requires the user to enter a year of birth that establishes they are 18 or older. This certification is a binding part of our Terms of Service, and misrepresenting one's age is a material breach that justifies immediate termination.

🚫 Never Marketed to Children

FlirtSpot is designed and marketed exclusively for adults. Our store listing, content, and targeting are not directed at, or appealing to, children, and we do not knowingly collect any data from anyone under 18.

🧹 If We Detect a Minor

If we learn that a user is under 18, we terminate their access immediately and purge their anonymous profile data in line with our data-retention and deletion practices. We rely on reports, behavioral signals, and platform tooling to surface these cases.

🔒 Anti-Capture Safeguards

We enforce Android's FLAG_SECURE on ephemeral views to disable screenshots and screen recording. This limits the spread of any captured media and helps protect everyone — most of all vulnerable users — from non-consensual redistribution. See our Privacy Promise for the full technical detail.

Report It. We Act Fast.

If you see a minor on the app, or any content exploiting a child, report it immediately. Here's what happens.

1

You Report It

Open the user's profile, tap Report User, and choose a reason — adding optional details if you like. Your report stays anonymous. You can also email our dedicated safety channel at safety@flirt.spot.

Underage User Child Safety Concern (CSAM) Harassment or Abuse Inappropriate Content Spam or Scams
“Report submitted. Thank you for keeping flirt.spot safe.”
2

We Triage & Remove

Child-safety reports are prioritized above all else. Confirmed violations lead to immediate content removal and account termination.

3

We Escalate to Authorities

Reports involving the sexual exploitation of a minor are escalated to the NCMEC CyberTipline and to the appropriate law-enforcement agencies, and evidence is preserved for their investigation.

4

We Block Re-entry

Banned users are blocked from creating new accounts. We continuously improve our detection so offenders cannot simply return under a new anonymous identity.

Authorities & Resources

If a child is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services right away.

🇺🇸

NCMEC CyberTipline

Report suspected child sexual exploitation to the U.S. National Center for Missing & Exploited Children. report.cybertip.org · 1-800-843-5678.

We comply with all applicable child safety laws and cooperate with NCMEC to report confirmed instances of CSAM.

🌍

International Reporting (INHOPE)

Outside the U.S., reports can be made through the INHOPE network of national hotlines at inhope.org, which routes CSAM reports to the correct country.

Contact Our Safety Team

For serious safety concerns or to report violations of our standards, contact our designated Safety Point of Contact at safety@flirt.spot. Urgent reports involving minors are handled as our top priority.

Child Safety is Everyone's Job

If you see something, report it — in-app or to safety@flirt.spot. There is no place for minors or child exploitation on FlirtSpot.
