What Data Does FlirtSpot Hold?

FlirtSpot uses anonymous accounts. We do not collect emails, phone numbers, social media profiles, or any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). The only data associated with your anonymous account is:

👤

Anonymous Username

A temporary, self-chosen display name. No real name required. Not linked to any external identity.

📸

Selfie Photo

A single selfie used for your anonymous profile. Stored encrypted on our servers and on your device.

🔐

Encrypted Session Keys

Cryptographic keys used for end-to-end encrypted communication. Stored locally and on the relay server during active sessions.

🚫

What We Do NOT Collect

No email addresses. No phone numbers. No social media links. No GPS location history. No IP logs. No cookies. No advertising IDs. No analytics profiles.

01

Automatic Purge

Because FlirtSpot is anonymous and does not use emails or passwords, all data is automatically and permanently purged from our servers after 24 hours of inactivity.

🔄 Automatic 🗑️ Permanent ⏱️ 24h after inactivity

This means you don't need to do anything for your data to be deleted. Simply stop using the app, and within 24 hours your anonymous username, selfie, and all session data will be permanently erased from our servers. No recovery possible.

02

Immediate Manual Deletion

Delete Profile Button

To delete your data immediately, use the 'Delete Profile' button inside the app Settings before uninstalling.

1

Open the FlirtSpot app.

2

Go to Settings (gear icon).

3

Tap 'Delete Profile'.

4

Confirm the deletion.

✅

This action immediately and permanently erases your anonymous username, selfie, encrypted session keys, and all associated data from both your device and our servers. This cannot be undone.

03

The No-Identity Clause

Since we do not collect PII (Personally Identifiable Information), we cannot verify identity via a web browser. If you have already uninstalled the app, rest assured your data will be wiped within the 24-hour automatic purge window.

❌

No Email-Based Deletion

We don't have your email, so we can't send you a deletion link.

❌

No Web Portal Login

There is no web dashboard because there are no accounts to log into.

❌

No Identity Verification

We can't verify who you are because we never knew who you were.

✅

Automatic 24h Purge

Already uninstalled? Your data self-destructs within 24 hours automatically.

This is by design. FlirtSpot's anonymity-first architecture means there is no way for anyone — including us — to associate your app data with your real-world identity. The trade-off is that data deletion can only be triggered from within the app, or by waiting for the automatic 24-hour purge.
